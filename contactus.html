Doyle

The station for the engine is located at a Carson City District BLM station in Doyle, CA. Office space is separate, but all other facilities are shared with the BLM. The engine is available through two dispatch centers. They are Plumas ECC located at the Supervisors Office in Quincy, CA; and Sierra Front Interagency Dispatch Center located in Minden, NV. This provides the opportunity for the engine to respond to incidents on lands administered by the Plumas N.F., Carson City District BLM, and the Humbolt-Toiyabe N.F. through-out Western Nevada and Northern California.
