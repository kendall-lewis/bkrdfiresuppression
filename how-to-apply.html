How to Apply

  1. Go to USAJobs.gov
  2. Click on "Advanced Search."
  3. Copy and paste the appropriate announcement number below for your desired grade level into the "Keyword Search" box.
  4. Click "Search Jobs."
  5. Click on the job description header.
  6. Click on the "Apply Online" button.

Job Types

Seasonal
Seasonal employment usually lasts from the beginning of May to October or November.

Apprenticeship
Before applying for the apprenticeship, it is recommended that you first complete a season or two with an engine module or a crew. The apprenticeship is a career-path oriented at promoting an individual to all aspects of fire suppression.

What is Needed

Besides the basic fire classes, the only thing that is needed is boots. We recommend either Whites, Nicks, or Drews. These boots are built tough for the job that is being performed. All other gear will be issued to you, however, it is your responsiblity to take care of that gear or you will end up buying it.

Veterans

Veterans are encouraged to apply. They are already familiar with the stucture of the forest service since it is similar to the military.
