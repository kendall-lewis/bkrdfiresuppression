Housing

There are no government houses available at any of the compounds. All compounds do have barracks available, except for Grizzly and Nervino - they use the barracks at Mohawk instead. Apartments are around, but might be hard to find for the season. Some people get apartments in Reno and commute. Find out more about the area here.
